This cake takes me back to mornings in Grandma’s kitchen, where the sweet aroma of toasted nuts and warm coffee filled the air.
Ingredients:
For the Cake:
1 cup ricotta cheese
½ cup almond flour
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup shelled pistachios, chopped (reserve some for topping)
½ cup granulated sugar
½ cup heavy cream or mascarpone cheese (for filling)
For the Almond Syrup Drizzle:
¼ cup powdered sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

**Step 1: Preheat and Prepare**
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan and line it with parchment paper.

**Tips for the Best Pistachio Almond Coffee Cake**
1. **Use Fresh Ingredients**: Fresh ricotta and high-quality pistachios make a big difference in flavor.
2. **Don’t Overmix**: Mix the batter just until combined to avoid a dense cake.
3. **Toast the Nuts**: Lightly toast the pistachios before chopping for extra flavor.
4. **Make It Ahead**: This cake can be made a day in advance and stored in an airtight container.
5. **Experiment with Fillings**: Try using whipped cream or a layer of fruit preserves for a different twist.

**Serving Suggestions**
