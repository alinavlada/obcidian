## **Day 1 – Full Body Strength & Stretching**
1. **Bodyweight Squats** – 3 sets | 8-10 reps | RPE 6
2. **Modified Push-ups (Knees or Wall)** – 3 sets | 6-8 reps | RPE 6
3. **Glute Bridges** – 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 6
4. **Cat-Cow Stretch** – 2 sets | 30 seconds | Focus on slow, deep breaths
5. **Child’s Pose** – 2 sets | 30 seconds | Hold and relax into the stretch

## **Day 2 – Core & Stretching Focus**
1. **Plank (Knees or Full)** – 3 sets | Hold for 20-30 seconds | RPE 6
2. **Bird-Dog** – 3 sets | 8-10 reps per side | RPE 6
3. **Side-Lying Leg Raises** – 2 sets | 10-12 reps per leg | RPE 6
4. **Seated Forward Fold** – 2 sets | 30 seconds | Focus on gentle reaching
5. **Seated Spinal Twist** – 2 sets | 30 seconds per side | Focus on stretching the spine

## **Day 3 – Upper Body & Gentle Stretching**
1. **Wall Push-ups** – 3 sets | 8-10 reps | RPE 6
2. **Dumbbell Rows (or use household objects like water bottles)** – 3 sets | 8-10 reps per arm | RPE 6
3. **Shoulder Shrugs (with light weights or water bottles)** – 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 6
4. **Standing Chest Stretch** – 2 sets | 30 seconds | Focus on opening the chest
5. **Overhead Arm Stretch** – 2 sets | 30 seconds | Hold and alternate arms

## **Day 4 – Lower Body & Stretching Focus**
1. **Lunges (use a chair for support if needed)** – 3 sets | 6-8 reps per leg | RPE 6
2. **Calf Raises (hold onto a chair or wall for support)** – 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 6
3. **Standing Hamstring Stretch** – 2 sets | 30 seconds per leg | Focus on gentle lengthening
4. **Hip Flexor Stretch** – 2 sets | 30 seconds per side | Focus on deep breathing
5. **Standing Quad Stretch** – 2 sets | 30 seconds per leg | Hold and gently pull the leg back
