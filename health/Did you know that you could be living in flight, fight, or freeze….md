**The Healing Power of Your Vagus Nerve**
The vagus nerve is part of your autonomic nervous system, which regulates all of your body’s basic functions. In this course, you’ll gain deep insights about your nervous system and the role it plays on healing chronic stress. Join holistic therapist Melissa Romano for a seven-day course designed to help you stimulate your vagus nerve and increase vagal tone. You’ll learn a toolkit of highly targeted exercises to effectively process and manage stress and your emotions, release stored trauma, decrease chronic pain, and begin living with intention and peace.
